Top 5 Blue Legacy Staples

Blue continues to be the best color in Legacy. With cards like Flusterstorm and the two Forces, blue provides clean answers to the degenerate combos prevalent in the format. The cantrips, Brainstorm and Ponder, make any deck more consistent by facilitating the ability to churn through your deck and find answers or threats as needed.

  1. Force of Will
  2. Brainstorm
  3. Ponder
  4. Force of Negation
  5. Flusterstorm
