Top 5 White Legacy Staples

White gets good removal with cards like Swords to Plowshares and Prismatic Ending. Timeless Dragon is a large, evasive finisher for control decks that has to be answered twice in most cases thanks to Eternalize. Deafening Silence is played in the sideboard to help against strategies that want to cast a flurry of spells in a single turn to win the game. Stoneforge Mystic is an old staple whose tutor ability continues to get more powerful with each new equipment that is released.

  1. Swords to Plowshares
  2. Prismatic Ending
  3. Timeless Dragon
  4. Deafening Silence
  5. Stoneforge Mystic
