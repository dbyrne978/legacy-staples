Top 5 Red Legacy Staples

It's easy to see how the power of blue has warped the format when the power of red comes from Pyroblast and Red Elemental Blast. Meltdown is a sideboard staple to answer narrow artifact strategies. Lightning Bolt continues to be the most efficient spell to deal damage as needed. Dragon's Rage Channeler is a newer threat that provides card selection with Surveil as well as a fast evasive threat when Delirium is active.

  1. Pyroblast
  2. Red Elemental Blast
  3. Meltdown
  4. Lightning Bolt
  5. Dragon's Rage Channeler
