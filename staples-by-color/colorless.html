Top 5 Colorless Legacy Staples

Lotus Petal is used in decks that want to power out fast starts. Mishra's Bauble is a card advantage neutral artifact that is played alongside Dragon's Rage Channeler to help enable Delirium. Pithing Needle, Grafdigger's Cage, and Shadowspear are all played as tutor targets for Urza's Saga.

  1. Lotus Petal
  2. Mishra's Bauble
  3. Pithing Needle
  4. Grafdigger's Cage
  5. Shadowspear
