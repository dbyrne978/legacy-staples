Top 5 Green Legacy Staples

Green gets to play newer cards like Endurance, Force of Vigor, and Collector Ouphe, designed to fight back against narrow strategies like artifacts and graveyards. Green Sun's Zenith is one of the best tutors available, allowing for green decks to run a varied toolbox of singleton green creatures. Carpet of Flowers is a reflection of how prevalent blue strategies are. When Carpet is in play, it is one of the best mana producers ever printed.

  1. Endurance
  2. Force of Vigor
  3. Collector Ouphe
  4. Green Sun's Zenith
  5. Carpet of Flowers
